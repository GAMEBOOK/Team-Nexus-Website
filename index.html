About Team Nexus.

Team Nexus is a group, not a clan, comprised of Annihilation, MineZ, HCF, and other members of the Shotbow community. We organize events, share videos, and play together on community hosted events. Discord is our main method of communication. Find new friends, play intense and friendly matches, or just chill out with other Shotbow players with Team Nexus! Join today!

Events

Team Nexus hosts a plethora of events frequently! These events are either ran by our organizers or community members like you. Furthermore, we aren't limited to only Shotbow games as we will schedule play-times on different servers and games!

Grow

Share your YouTube, Twitch Livestrams, and other content in our bustling media channel! We encourage you to post your epic Shotbow experiences as well as other gameplay from your favorite network(s). You can also find other YouTubers to collaborate with and find enjoyable content from other members of the community.

Community Ran

Team Nexus is ran by the community for the community! We encourage members to have an active say in how our server is ran, after all, it is just a bunch of gamers who want to have fun! Connect with clan members from Shotbow and start playing!
